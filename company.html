We're on a mission to make 3D AI as accessible as 2D image generation.

Our Mission

After 13 years in AI research, we've watched AI master text (ChatGPT), images (Midjourney), and video (Sora) — but not 3D.

Apple's Vision Pro and Meta Quest made it clear: spatial computing will be next.

We're building the foundational software and APIs for spatial intelligence — enabling developers to reconstruct, edit, and generate navigable 3D worlds as easily as they work with 2D images today.

Why Now?

1. Market Validation

Apple and Meta have validated spatial computing as a market with Vision Pro and Quest 3. The hardware is here — now we need the software infrastructure.

2. Technology Breakthrough

Gaussian Splatting (2023) made real-time 3D reconstruction feasible. Neural rendering and diffusion models can now extend to spatial generation.

3. AI Maturity

Generative AI technology is mature enough to extend beyond 2D. Transformers, diffusion models, and neural rendering converge for 3D.

Founding Technical Team

Our founding engineers are PhD candidates specializing in spatial AI, 3D reconstruction, gaussian splatting, and generative modeling. Deep domain expertise in computer vision and neural rendering.
